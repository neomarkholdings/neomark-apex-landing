Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Neomark Holdings LLC ("Neomark," "we," "us") collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards personal data in connection with our website and the Neomark Apex Medicaid and Medicare formulary-intelligence data product (collectively, the "Services"). It applies to all Consumer Personal Data we collect or that is collected on our behalf.

1. Who we are

Neomark Holdings LLC is a healthcare data-infrastructure company. We aggregate and standardize publicly published state Medicaid Preferred Drug Lists and federal Medicare Part D formulary reference data into verified, NDC-level datasets delivered to business customers via the Snowflake Marketplace and native data shares.

2. The data in our product is not personal data

The formulary dataset we publish is derived solely from publicly available government and payer drug-coverage sources (state Medicaid PDLs, CMS reference files, the FDA NDC Directory, and the NIH RxNorm terminology). It describes drug coverage rules — brand/generic names, NDCs, tiers, prior-authorization status, and coverage criteria. It contains no patient, member, or other individual health information.

3. Personal data we collect

  • Contact & account data you provide — name, business email, company, and role — when you request access, subscribe via the Snowflake Marketplace, or contact us.
  • Billing data processed by our payment and marketplace providers (Snowflake and Stripe); we do not store full payment-card numbers.
  • Usage & technical data — basic, aggregated analytics such as pages viewed and approximate region. Our public landing pages use no third-party advertising trackers.

4. How we use personal data

  • To provide, deliver, and support the Services and respond to your requests.
  • To process subscriptions, billing, and account administration.
  • To send service and transactional communications, and (only with your consent) product updates.
  • To secure, maintain, and improve the Services and comply with legal obligations.

5. How we share personal data

We do not sell personal data. We share it only with:

  • Service providers / sub-processors that operate the Services on our behalf, including Snowflake Inc. (data delivery & marketplace) and Stripe, Inc. (payment processing).
  • Legal & safety recipients where required by law or to protect rights, safety, and the integrity of the Services.
  • Business transfers in connection with a merger, acquisition, or asset sale.

6. Data security & retention

We apply administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards appropriate to the data we hold, including encryption in transit, least-privilege access controls, and key-pair authentication for production data pipelines. We retain personal data only as long as needed for the purposes above or as required by law.

7. Your privacy rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (including under the CCPA/CPRA and GDPR), you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or port your personal data, and to opt out of certain processing. To exercise any right, contact us at privacy@neomarkholdings.com. We will respond within the timeframes required by applicable law.

8. International data

We operate in the United States. If you access the Services from outside the U.S., you understand your personal data may be processed in the U.S. under this Policy.

9. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, by additional notice.
